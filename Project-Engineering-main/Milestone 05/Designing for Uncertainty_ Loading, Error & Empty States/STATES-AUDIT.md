## Loading Strategy
Skeleton UI is used for lists because it preserves layout and improves perceived performance.

## Error Messages
Orders: We couldn’t load your orders. Check your connection and try again.  
Products: We couldn’t load products. Try again.  
Customers: Unable to fetch customers. Please retry.  

## Empty States
Orders: No orders yet — appears for new users  
Products: No products — CTA to add product  
Customers: No customers — informational
